# Public and private layers

This repository exists because the project needs a public research layer without exposing the whole process.

## Private layer

The private layer includes:

- private conversations;
- e-mails;
- WhatsApp messages;
- institutional negotiations;
- budgets and procurement details;
- internal presentations;
- unpublished texts not cleared for release;
- production contacts;
- unresolved interpersonal decisions.

This material does not belong in the public repository.

## Research archive layer

The research archive may include:

- conceptual notes written for public release;
- sanitized apparatus diagrams;
- public references;
- technical sketches;
- redacted field notes;
- general system architecture;
- archive and preservation policy;
- code or prototypes only when safe to publish.

## Artwork layer

The final artwork may include elements not present here, and may contradict early research notes.

The repository should not freeze the work prematurely.

## Rule

```txt
If publishing a fragment would expose a person, negotiation, budget, institution, or unresolved authorship boundary, it stays out.
```
