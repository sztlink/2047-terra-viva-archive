# Redaction policy

This repository follows a conservative redaction policy.

## Redact by default

Do not publish:

- private messages;
- e-mail excerpts;
- budgets;
- supplier names when linked to negotiation;
- institutional documents;
- unreleased presentation material;
- calendar details;
- private contact information;
- raw prompts that expose unpublished dramaturgy;
- generated outputs that could function as premature artwork release.

## Safe to publish after review

Potentially safe:

- general apparatus descriptions;
- diagrams redrawn from scratch;
- public references;
- high-level technical notes;
- field notes without private detail;
- code that does not contain credentials, private data, or institutional material.

## Authorship review

Because the artwork is collaborative, conceptual framing should be reviewed before major public updates.

Technical notes maintained by Felipe can evolve faster, but they should not redefine the shared artwork without explicit review.

## Timing review

Before public releases, check whether publication might interfere with:

- institutional announcement timing;
- curatorial review;
- funding or procurement;
- authorship clarity;
- the work's right to remain unfinished.
